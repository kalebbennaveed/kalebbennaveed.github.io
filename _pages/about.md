I am a senior at [The Hong Kong Polytechnic University](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/en/) interested in pursuing a research career in robotic decision making and artificial intelligence. I’m currently interested in working on Reinforcement Learning (RL) and Meta RL with a goal of improving the capability of intelligent agents to efficiently learn generalizable, task adaptive, optimal, and safe behavior policies in uncertain surroundings.

Currently, I am doing research on Behavior planning for Autonomous Vehicles Using Multi Agent RL under guidance of [Dr. Edward Chung](http://www.ee.polyu.edu.hk/en/people_detail.php?name=UlasKARAAGAC&cid=1&id=26002) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Previously, I have also collaborated with [Dr. John Dolan](https://www.ri.cmu.edu/ri-faculty/john-m-dolan/) and [Dr. Sebastian Scherer](https://www.ri.cmu.edu/ri-faculty/sebastian-scherer/) from [The Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University](https://www.ri.cmu.edu/). I had the opportunity to work on several projects relating to hierarchical reinforcement learning, trajectory planning, informative planning using optimization, and intrusion detection.
